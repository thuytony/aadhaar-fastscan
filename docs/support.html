How to Use

  1. Open the app and tap Scan QR Code.
  2. Allow camera access when prompted.
  3. Align the QR code on your Aadhaar card within the frame.
  4. Hold steady until the code is detected.

Alternatively, tap Choose from Photos to scan a QR code from a saved image.

Common Issues

Camera permission denied
Go to iPhone Settings → Aadhaar FastScan → Camera → enable access.

QR code not detected
Ensure good lighting, hold the phone steady, use the flash if needed, and try pinch-to-zoom. Dense Aadhaar QR codes may take a few seconds to decode.

“Not a valid Aadhaar QR”
Make sure you are scanning the QR code printed on an official Aadhaar card (legacy XML or Secure QR format).

No internet required
The app works fully offline. No account or network connection is needed.
