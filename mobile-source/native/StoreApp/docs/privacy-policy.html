Privacy Policy

Aadhaar FastScan · Last updated: June 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Aadhaar FastScan (“the App”), developed by Techlimo, handles information when you use the App on your device.

Summary

The App scans QR codes printed on Aadhaar cards and displays decoded information on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your personal information. All processing happens locally on your device.

Information the App Processes

When you scan an Aadhaar QR code, the App may decode and display fields such as name, date of birth, gender, address, and a masked Aadhaar number. This information is shown only on your device during your session. The App does not persistently store this data and does not send it to any server.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration or login is required
  • No data is uploaded to our servers or any third party
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used
  • No internet connection is required for core app functionality

Device Permissions

Camera: Used only to scan QR codes in real time. Camera images are processed on your device and are not saved or transmitted by the App.

Photo Library: Used only when you choose to scan a QR code from a saved image. Selected images are processed locally and are not stored or transmitted by the App.

Data Retention

Scanned information exists only while you use the App. When you close the App or start a new scan, previously displayed information is not retained by the App.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party services that collect personal data.

Disclaimer

Aadhaar FastScan is an independent utility app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by UIDAI (Unique Identification Authority of India) or the Government of India.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
